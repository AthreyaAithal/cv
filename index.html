Professional Summary

Driven by Quality.

I'm a senior engineering professional who has spent over a decade inside one of the world's most technically demanding software ecosystems, building quality engineering capability from the ground up across multiple generations of platform SDK programs. My career began in compiler development using Clang-LLVM infrastructure, which gave me a rare low-level systems foundation that informs everything I do in validation, automation, and framework design.

Over the years, my role has grown from hands-on execution into technical ownership, strategic quality direction, and framework-level architecture. I believe great quality engineering is not about catching bugs — it's about building systems that prevent regressions at scale, enable developer productivity, and create lasting improvements to engineering maturity across the organization.
